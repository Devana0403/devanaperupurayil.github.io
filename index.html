01 — About

Designing with data,
engineering with intent.

I'm a Data Scientist and Software Engineer at the University of California, San Diego, where I study in the Halicioğlu Data Science Institute. My work sits at the crossroads of machine learning, natural language processing, and data systems — building tools that turn messy, real-world data into decisions that matter.

From building healthcare graph databases to training prosody-aware speech models, I approach every project with a bias toward clarity: clear hypotheses, clean pipelines, and rigorous evaluation. I believe the best data science is inseparable from good engineering.

Outside of research, I'm interested in scalable AI oversight, statistical modelling, and making complex systems legible to the humans who depend on them.

Physics · ML · Bayesian Inference

MJD Data Release — Neutrinoless Decay Analysis

Applied MCMC Bayesian parameter estimation (emcee, 50 walkers × 10k steps) to the Majorana Demonstrator detector dataset to constrain the signal count of neutrinoless double beta decay. Achieved TPR = 0.801, AUC = 0.86, and a 90% confidence upper limit of θ_NLDBD < 7.97.

  • Python
  • emcee
  • SciPy
  • MCMC
  • Bayesian Stats
Manifold Learning · Spectral Methods

Noise-Adaptive Spectral Embedding for Diffusion Maps

Implemented and evaluated three data-driven truncation strategies for diffusion maps under additive noise across 120 seeded runs on 5 synthetic manifolds. Bandwidth stability outperformed the eigengap heuristic by +3.5 pp trustworthiness on the S-curve and showed zero seed variance on uniform-curvature manifolds.

  • NumPy
  • Diffusion Maps
  • Spectral Methods
  • Manifold Learning
Statistical Modelling · R

Building Cooling Load Modelling

Fitted multiple linear regression and spline-enhanced models to predict building cooling loads from 8 architectural features. Used stepwise AIC selection, residual diagnostics, bootstrapped standard errors, and natural splines — improving adjusted R² from 0.889 to 0.967 and cutting residual standard error by 45%.
